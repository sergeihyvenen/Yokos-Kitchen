About Yoko's Kitchen

Tradition and passion for Japanese cuisine

Yoko's Kitchen was created to bring authentic Japanese cooking to Tallinn. Our goal is to share traditional recipes, fresh ingredients, and unique cooking techniques with everyone who loves food and wants to learn something new.

Our chef has years of experience in Japanese cuisine and enjoys teaching students in a simple and practical way. Every lesson is designed to be easy to follow and enjoyable for all skill levels.

Our Mission

Quality, learning, and experience

We believe that cooking should be both fun and meaningful. At Yoko's Kitchen, students not only learn recipes but also understand the culture behind Japanese food.

Whether you join our classes or order catering, we always aim to provide high-quality dishes and a great experience. Our kitchen combines traditional methods with modern ideas to create something special.

Popular Recipes

Yakitori (grilled chicken) Tsukune (minced meat patties) Okonomiyaki (savory pancakes) Mizutaki (chicken stew)
