Mizutaki

Traditional Japanese chicken hot pot

Mizutaki is a simple and healthy Japanese chicken soup often served as a hot pot dish. It is especially popular during colder seasons.

The dish is known for its light but rich flavor, made by slowly cooking chicken and vegetables in broth.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Vegetables
  • Water or broth
  • Tofu
  • Green onions

Preparation:

Cook chicken and vegetables in a pot with water or broth until tender. Serve hot and enjoy with dipping sauces if desired.

Popular Recipes

Yakitori (grilled chicken) Tsukune (minced meat patties) Okonomiyaki (savory pancakes) Mizutaki (chicken stew)
