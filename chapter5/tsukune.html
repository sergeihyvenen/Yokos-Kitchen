Tsukune

Japanese chicken meatballs

Tsukune are Japanese-style chicken meatballs often served on skewers. They are juicy, soft, and full of flavor, usually glazed with a sweet soy sauce.

This dish is popular in Japanese restaurants and is often enjoyed as a snack or part of a larger meal.

Ingredients:

  • Minced chicken
  • Egg
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Green onions

Preparation:

Mix all ingredients together, form small balls, and cook them on a grill or pan. Brush with sauce while cooking for extra flavor.
