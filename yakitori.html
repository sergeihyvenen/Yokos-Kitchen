Yakitori

Traditional Japanese grilled chicken

Yakitori is a popular Japanese dish made from small pieces of chicken grilled on skewers. It is usually cooked over charcoal and seasoned with salt or a special sweet soy sauce called tare.

This dish is commonly served in Japanese street food stalls and restaurants. It is simple, delicious, and perfect for sharing with friends or enjoying as a quick meal.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken meat
  • Soy sauce
  • Sugar
  • Garlic
  • Wooden skewers

Preparation:

Cut the chicken into small pieces and place them on skewers. Grill the meat while brushing it with sauce until it becomes golden and juicy. Serve hot.

Popular Recipes

Yakitori (grilled chicken) Tsukune (minced meat patties) Okonomiyaki (savory pancakes) Mizutaki (chicken stew)
